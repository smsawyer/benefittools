This tool is provided as a courtesy. Contract rules supersede this tool. Refer to your current Group Master Application or contract to determine the employee's probationary period.

* Event date / date of hire: The date the employee has satisfied all eligibility criteria and is eligible to begin serving the probationary period. Refer to your contract and/or Group Administrator Guide for eligibility guidelines.

** Date of hire: For members serving a First of the Month Following Date of Hire probationary period, refer to your Group Master Application for the appropriate selection. Employees hired on the first of the month:

  1. Effective date = date of hire, or
  2. Effective date = the first of the month following their date of hire.

*** Custom probationary periods: Available only to groups rated 101+ and must be selected and approved at setup or renewal.
